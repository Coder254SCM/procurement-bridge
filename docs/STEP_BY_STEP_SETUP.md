## Getting Started - For Complete Beginners

### What is the Kenya e-GP Platform?

The Kenya e-Government Procurement Platform is a **complete digital solution** that replaces paper-based procurement processes with a secure, transparent, and efficient online system. Think of it as the "digital transformation" of how government and organizations buy goods and services.

#### What Makes It Special?
- **Blockchain Technology**: Every action is recorded permanently and cannot be changed
- **AI-Powered Analytics**: Smart recommendations and risk assessment
- **Complete Integration**: Works with existing systems like SAP, IFMIS, M-Pesa
- **Government Compliant**: Meets all Kenya PPRA requirements
- **Enterprise Security**: Bank-level security with multiple layers of protection

## Common Issues and Troubleshooting

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### **Q: I can't log in to the system**
**A:** Follow these steps:
1. Check your email for the verification link
2. Ensure you're using the correct email and password
3. Try resetting your password using "Forgot Password"
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. Contact support if issues persist

#### **Q: My documents won't upload**
**A:** Common solutions:
1. Check file size (maximum 10MB per file)
2. Ensure file format is supported (PDF, DOC, XLS, JPG, PNG)
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Disable browser extensions temporarily

#### **Q: I don't see any tenders to bid on**
**A:** Check these items:
1. Ensure your supplier profile is complete
2. Verify your business category matches tenders
3. Check that you're verified at the required level
4. Look in the correct geographic region
5. Ensure your account has necessary permissions

#### **Q: Integration with our ERP system isn't working**
**A:** Troubleshooting steps:
1. Verify API credentials are correct
2. Check network connectivity and firewall settings
3. Validate data mapping configuration
4. Test with a small sample dataset
5. Contact technical support for assistance
